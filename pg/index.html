Common Questions

MBA requires a graduate degree in any discipline from a recognized university. MCA requires a graduate degree with Mathematics or Computer Science as a subject at 10+2 or graduation level.

The MBA program fee is ₹1,00,000 per year and the MCA program fee is ₹50,000 per year, both for the 2-year postgraduate program. Fees may be subject to revision — please confirm with our admissions team.

M.Tech requires a B.Tech/B.E. degree in a relevant discipline. The annual fee is ₹60,000 for the 2-year program, with specialisations available in Computer Science Engineering and AI & Machine Learning.
